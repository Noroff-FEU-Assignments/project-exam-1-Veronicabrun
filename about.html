ABOUT ME

Welcome to Design Studio!

Hello and thank you for visiting my interior design blog! I'm Amanda, and I'm deeply passionate about all things interior design. As an interior design enthusiast and blogger, I aim to share my passion, inspiration, and knowledge with you.

Interior design has been a lifelong passion for me. From decorating my own room as a child to exploring various design styles and trends, I've seen the impact of a well-designed home on mood and productivity.

On this blog, you'll find a variety of content, from DIY projects to budget-friendly design tips. Whether you're an experienced designer or just starting out, I hope you'll find something valuable here.

Warm regards
